Sushi Stacks with Salmon Patties and Cucumber Salad

Anda bisa membuat Sushi Stacks with Salmon Patties and Cucumber Salad memakai 27 jenis bahan dan 4 langkah pembuatan. Berikut ini cara dalam membuat hidangannya.

Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat Sushi Stacks with Salmon Patties and Cucumber Salad:

  1. Sushi Rice:
  2. 1 cup Short grain rice
  3. 1.5 cups Water
  4. 2 tbsp Rice vinegar
  5. 1 tbsp Water
  6. 1 tbsp Sugar
  7. 3/4 tsp Salt
  8. Salmon Patties:
  9. 300 gr Skinless salmon fillet
  10. 1/2 pcs Small onion, diced
  11. 2 cm Ginger, fine chopped
  12. 1/4 cup Bread crumbs
  13. 1/2 tbsp Roasted black sesame seeds
  14. 1 pcs Egg
  15. 1/2 tbsp Miso
  16. 1 tbsp Honey
  17. 1/2 tbsp Rice vinegar
  18. Salad with Sesame Salad Dressing:
  19. 1 pcs Small english cucumber, small cubed
  20. 1/4 pcs Red bell pepper, small cubed
  21. 1/2 tbsp Roasted black sesame seeds
  22. 2 tbsp Roasted white sesame seeds, ground
  23. 2 tbsp Mayonaise
  24. 1 tbsp Sugar
  25. 1/2 tbsp Soy sauce
  26. 1/2 tbsp Rice vinegar
  27. 1/2 tbsp Sesame oil

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat Sushi Stacks with Salmon Patties and Cucumber Salad

1
Sesame Salad Dressing: Mix together ground roasted white sesame, mayonaise, rice vinegar, soy sauce, sugar and salt until well combined. Put aside.
2
Salmon Patties: Cut salmon fillets into 0.6 cm cubes, put 2/3 of cubed salmon in the mixing bowl. Chop the remaining 1/3 of cubed salmon into fine paste, then put into the mixing bowl. Mix salmon, diced onion, chopped ginger, egg, roasted black sesame seeds, bread crumbs, miso, honey and rice vinegar until well combined and it can be formed into balls. Heat frypan on medium-low heat with canola oil and cook the patties until the first side is golden brown, then flip cook through.
3
Sushi Rice: Make sushi seasoning: rice vinegar, 1 tbsp water, sugar and salt. Put aside. Cook rice and 1.5 cup of water in the rice cooker. Once it cooked, seasoned and mix the rice with sushi seasoning.
4
Finishing: Combine cubed cucumber, red bell pepper and toasted black sesame seeds with sesame salad dressing. Mold sushi rice into ball and press it, then topped the sushi rice with salad and salmon patty.
