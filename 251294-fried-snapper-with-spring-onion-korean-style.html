Fried snapper with spring onion korean style

Dipos pada March 28, 2022

Tetap dengan bahan yang sederhana, sajian ini bisa memberi manfaat dalam membantu menjaga kesehatan tubuh kita. Anda bisa membuat Fried snapper with spring onion korean style memakai 16 jenis bahan dan 3 langkah pembuatan. Berikut ini cara dalam membuat hidangannya.

Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat Fried snapper with spring onion korean style:

  1. Ingredients:
  2. 1 Whole Black or Red Snapper, washed and spread a pinch of salt all over the fish and put in the fridge while we preparing the sauce
  3. 1/3 cup cooking oil to fry the fish later
  4. 2 tablespoon plain flour
  5. 2 teaspoons salt
  6. The Sauce:
  7. 1/4 cup soy sauce
  8. 3 stalks spring onion, chopped
  9. 4 garlic cloves, finely minced
  10. 1 green chili pepper, chopped
  11. 1 tablespoon hot pepper flakes
  12. 2 long red chili, chopped (Optional)
  13. 1 tablespoon hot pepper flakes
  14. 2 teaspoons white sugar
  15. 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  16. 1 tablespoons roasted sesame seeds

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat Fried snapper with spring onion korean style

1
Mixed all the seasoning sauce in a bowl and put aside.
2
Pour the flour on both sides of the fish. Heat up a nonstick pan and add the cooking oil.
3
Cook the fish until golden brown on both side. It will roughly take 20 minutes in total. Once cooked, put in the plate and pour the sauce on top of the fish while it is still hot so it will soaked all the sauce goodness! Enjoy!
